How to Play


The purpose of this game is for the player to match pairs of cards against a clock, the taget will be displayed underneath the cards and it is the players job to match pairs and hit execute before the clock runs out. The Target is generated Randomly and so is the clock so dont get too comfortable! You will need to match pairs in the timeframe to increase your bank, and if your too slow you'll end up broke! Players Win when you double your starting Bank balance! Good Luck!
