The FinTech Card Game

How to Play


The purpose of this game is for the player to match pairs of cards against a clock, the taget will be displayed underneath the cards and it is the players job to match pairs and hit execute before the clock runs out. The Target is generated Randomly and so is the clock you could have anywhere from 100 seconds to 1 second and you could have the same target multiple times in a row so dont get too comfortable! You will need to match pairs within the time to increase your bank, and if your too slow you'll end up broke! Players Win when you double your starting Bank balance! Good Luck!
