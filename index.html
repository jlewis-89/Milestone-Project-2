How to Play

The fintech card game has been designed to create a high-pressure card matching environment. *You must match the card pair to the ticker shown below within the time limit.
*You will start with $1000 in the bank and every correct match gains you $100 to your bank, every incorrect match will loose you $100.
* To win you must double your bank, but be warned the timer is random, sometimes you will have plenty of time others you will have only seconds once the timer hits 0 you will loose $100 every second you fail to find the matching pair.
*The deck will reshuffle after every found pair, the timer will reset and so will the target ticker.
*There are 3 tickers that can be shown, and these are selected at random.
*YOU MAY GET THE SAME TICKER MULTIPLE TIMES...
*When you are ready click “START” and Good Luck!
