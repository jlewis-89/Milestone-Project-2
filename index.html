The FinTech Card Investment Game

How to Play


The purpose of this game is for the player to purchase 4 out of the 8 cards available and accumulate as much bank as possible from picking the best performing stocks. Each card will be populated by a figure, and after selecting the deck, an API call will evaluate the stock price and return either an increase or a loss depending on the stocks performance, if you pick well you will increase your overall bank, however if you pick poorly you could loose it all!
